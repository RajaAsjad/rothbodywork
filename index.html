About

Who is Courtney Roth?

Courtney Roth is a California-licensed massage therapist who specializes in medical massage — working with people who have pain, structural imbalances, and soft tissue injuries.

She combines deep tissue massage, Active Release Therapy (ART), Thai massage, and stress-relief techniques to reduce pain and restore flexibility. With 34 years of experience, Courtney uses intuition and technical skill to deliver results that last.

Every session is collaborative: you set the goal, she builds the treatment. Clients leave with greater mobility, comfort, and value for their investment in their health.
